# Adidas Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
A comprehensive business intelligence dashboard for Adidas sales performance, providing insights across products, retailers, regions, and time periods. This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making through multi-dimensional sales analysis.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Executive Summary Metrics
- **Total Revenue**: $899,902,120
- **Total Transactions**: 9,648
- **Total Units Sold**: 2,478,861
- **Operating Profit**: $332,134,761
- **Average Order Value**: $93,273
- **Profit Margin**: 37%

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Product Sales Analysis
**Chart Type**: Vertical bar chart

Displays sales performance by product category across different months (Men's and Women's categories):
- Men's Apparel
- Men's Athletic Footwear
- Men's Street Footwear
- Women's Apparel
- Women's Athletic Footwear
- Women's Street Footwear

**Peak Performance**: Men's Athletic Footwear shows the highest sales volumes, reaching over $200,000,000 in certain months.

### 2. Retailer Profit Analysis
**Chart Type**: Horizontal bar chart

Profit breakdown by retailer partner:
- **Amazon**: $20,723,135
- **Foot Locker**: $96,311,259
- **Kohl's**: $74,322,035
- **Sports Direct**: $55,783,053
- **Walmart**: [Value shown in chart]
- **West Gear**: $85,667,873

**Top Performer**: Foot Locker leads with approximately $96.3M in profit.

### 3. Sales Method Performance
**Chart Type**: Vertical bar chart

Compares sales across distribution channels:
- **In-store**: Significant volume
- **Online**: Strong performance with high sales
- **Outlet**: Competitive sales numbers

All three channels show robust performance over $200M, with Online and Outlet showing particularly strong results.

### 4. Monthly Sales Trend
**Chart Type**: Line graph

Year-over-year trend analysis showing:
- Sales fluctuations throughout the year (January to December)
- Peak sales periods around July
- Seasonal variations in sales performance
- Overall trend line for total sales
- Scale ranges from $0 to $120,000,000

## Key Insights

### Performance Highlights
1. **Strong Overall Performance**: Nearly $900M in total revenue with a healthy 37% profit margin
2. **High Transaction Volume**: 9,648 transactions with an impressive average order value of $93,273
3. **Retailer Diversity**: Multiple retail partners contributing to sales distribution
4. **Balanced Channel Mix**: All three sales methods (in-store, online, outlet) performing well
5. **Geographic Spread**: Sales distributed across all major US regions

### Strategic Observations
- **Product Mix**: Men's Athletic Footwear is the star performer
- **Retail Partnership**: Foot Locker is the most profitable retail partner
- **Regional Focus**: Midwest represents the largest market opportunity
- **Seasonal Trends**: Mid-year shows peak sales performance
- **Digital Presence**: Online sales channel shows strong performance
